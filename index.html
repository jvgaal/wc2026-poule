📖 How to Play
Everything you need to know about the WC 2026 Poule
⚽
Group Stage

Predict the exact score for all 48 group stage matches. You earn:

  • 3 points — Exact score (e.g. 2-1)
  • 1 point — Correct result (W/D/L)

Matches are locked round-by-round as they start. You can adjust predictions up until kick-off.

🏆
Knockout Rounds

After group results are in, predict the full knockout bracket:

  • Round of 32 — Pick the winner (+2 pts)
  • Round of 16 — Pick the winner (+3 pts)
  • Quarter-Finals — Pick the winner (+4 pts)
  • Semi-Finals — Pick the winner (+5 pts)
  • 3rd Place Match — Pick the winner (+3 pts)
  • Final — Pick the winner (+8 pts)

Winners cascade automatically — picking a Round of 32 winner auto-fills the Round of 16 slot.

⭐
Bonus Questions

10 special predictions that can make or break your ranking:

  • Tournament winner, runner-up
  • Golden Boot, Golden Ball, Golden Glove
  • Best Young Player
  • Total goals in tournament (within range)
  • First team to be eliminated
  • Red card in the Final
  • Top scoring team

Points range from 1 to 10 per question — these can swing the leaderboard!

🔒
Prediction Deadlines

Predictions are locked by round:

  • Group Stage — Locked at kick-off (June 11, 2026)
  • Round of 32 — June 28
  • Round of 16 — July 5
  • Quarter-Finals — July 10
  • Semi-Finals — July 14
  • Final — July 19

Once a round locks, you can no longer edit predictions for that round.

📊
Scoring Overview

Maximum 358 points available:

  • Group Stage — up to 216 pts (3 pts × 48 exact + 1 pt × 48 result)
  • Knockout — up to 93 pts
  • Bonus Questions — up to 49 pts

Your score updates live after each match result is entered. Keep an eye on the leaderboard!
